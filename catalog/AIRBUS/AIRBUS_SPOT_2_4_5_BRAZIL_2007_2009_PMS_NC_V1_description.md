This collection provides high-resolution pansharpened pseudo-natural color imagery. Where a corresponding high-resolution panchromatic image was available, it was fused with multispectral data to achieve enhanced spatial detail (up to 5m for SPOT 5 or 10m for SPOT 2/4). These products are optimized for identifying "consolidated areas" of human occupation and deforestation prior to the 2008 Brazil Forest Code deadline.

Google Modifications: Addition of a cloud mask band. Google manually delineated polygons around clouds, wisps, and shadows for each image in this collection, converting the polygons into a mask image added as an additional band to the image.
