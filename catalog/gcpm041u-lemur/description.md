Suite of nationally consistent, rigorously validated datasets describing multiple Canadian forest attributes. These high-quality datasets were developed by the remote sensing team at the [Laurentian Forestry Centre](https://natural-resources.canada.ca/science-data/science-research/research-centres/laurentian-forestry-centre) of the [Canadian Forest Service](https://natural-resources.canada.ca/corporate/corporate-overview/canadian-forest-service), [Natural Resources Canada](https://www.nrcan.gc.ca/home).
